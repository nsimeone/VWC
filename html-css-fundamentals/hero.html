My name is Nick, I am in Seattle, WA. I love hockey, the community is one of the best communities I have ever been apart of. It seems super tight-knit, I like how you can reach out to a teammate for anything and they will always be there for you. I started playing later in life, and it was one of the best decisions I have ever made. You are never too old to start and I try to apply the mentality to everything I do in life.


When I am not playing hockey I am running. I have a goal to run around the entirety of Lake Washington which will be about 63 miles. I have injured myself the last two years trying to achieve this goal, but I am hoping the third time is the charm! I plan on visiting Europe soon, I would love to play hockey over there for a year - I have been leaning towards playing in Paris or Madrid My Spanish is terrible, but my french is non-existent so I am leaning more towards Madrid. I would also like to return to Argentina or Mexico.
